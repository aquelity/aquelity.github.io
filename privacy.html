Across our products and websites, we may collect:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and authentication credentials when you register

The specific data collected by each product is detailed in that product's supplemental privacy notice.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our products and services

  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service-related communications
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations
  • Understand how our products are used and improve them

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information in transit and at rest. Our primary servers are located in China. For international users, data may be processed in other jurisdictions; we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any such transfers.

No security system is completely impenetrable. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Third parties who assist in operating our services, such as cloud infrastructure and payment processors, under strict data processing agreements

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data; to withdraw consent for data processing; and to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@aquelity.com.

Our websites use only essential functional cookies necessary for operation. We do not use advertising, behavioral, or cross-site tracking cookies. Individual products may use local storage mechanisms; details are provided in each product's supplemental privacy notice.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at legal@aquelity.com.
